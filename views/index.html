about us

BlockTrain is a global blockchain media platform serving North America and Greater China markets. BlockTrain is dedicated to providing blockchain education and insights through various media channels by curating technology reviews, interviewing industry leaders, organizing development workshops, providing online training, and hosting global conferences.

what we do

BlockTrain News & Reviews

BlockTrain produces news and reviews on the cutting edge of the blockchain technology and its applications in various industries. BlockTrain develops the critical insights for the global blockchain community in terms of technology, application, market, and regulation.

BlockTrain Talk

BlockTrain is a video content pioneer in the blockchain community. The founder of BlockTrain has produced interviews with many blockchain projects, such as DFINITY, R3, Bitcoin Gold, Veem, ConsenSys, Hash World, and Trade Terminal. BlockTrain aims to promote the blockchain technology through our original video series.

BlockTrain Training

BlockTrain has been developing a platform that provides training on the distributed ledger technology. Partnering with world-class academic institutions, we offer video courses on the most up-to-date blockchain subjects, tailoring training sessions for both individuals and corporate organizations.

BlockTrain PR Service

BlockTrain develops custom public relations strategies to promote blockchain projects in the United States and China. BlockTrain offers a variety of services, including mainstream media coverage, branding within the blockchain community, event planning, and media content production.

Meet The Team

Zhang, Mengfan

Mengfan Zhang is the founder of BlockTrain. Mengfan started her career in the blockchain media space since October 2016 when she joined the North America Blockchain Association as the Vice President of Media. She is one of the earliest media pioneers in the blockchain community in the U.S. and China. Since then, she has developed media and PR strategies strategies and produced exclusive video and verbal contents for a series of blockchain projects, such as DFINITY, R3, Bitcoin Gold, Veem, ConsenSys, Hash World, and Trade Terminal. She has been the organizer of several popular Blockchain events in Silicon Valley and was in charge of promoting and organizing the NABA Silicon Valley Fintech Blockchain summit in Jan 2017 which attracted 600+ attendees and 50+ well known speakers. Mengfan was dedicated to Blockchain information, resources and knowledge exchange between China and US.

Global Blockchain Technology Summit

BlockTrain and Draper Dragon Fund are hosting the Global Blockchain Technology Summit on May 6th, 2018 at the Santa Clara Convention Center in Silicon Valley, USA. This summit will be the first of its kind focusing on the distributed ledger technology projects.
